model-server installed as release "{{ .Release.Name }}".

Local access:
  kubectl -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} port-forward svc/{{ include "model-server.fullname" . }} 8000:{{ .Values.service.port }}
  open http://localhost:8000/docs

Endpoints:
  GET  /health    liveness/readiness (503 until the model is loaded)
  GET  /metrics   Prometheus exposition (scraped by the monitoring ServiceMonitor)
  POST /predict   run an inference
  POST /admin/reload-model   (Bearer ADMIN_TOKEN) re-pull the artifact from S3

Reminders:
  - This namespace needs the "{{ .Values.s3.existingSecret }}" Secret (S3 creds).
  - The model artifact "{{ .Values.config.modelKey }}" must exist in the models
    bucket, otherwise /health reports degraded until Airflow trains one.
