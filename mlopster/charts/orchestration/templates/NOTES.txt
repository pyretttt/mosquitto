Before the training DAG can run:
  1. Copy the S3 credentials Secret into this namespace ({{ .Release.Namespace }}):
       kubectl -n storage get secret s3-credentials -o yaml \
         | sed 's/namespace: storage/namespace: {{ .Release.Namespace }}/' \
         | kubectl apply -f -
  2. Make sure git-sync points at your repo (airflow.dags.gitSync.repo) so
     airflow/dags/train_model.py is synced in.
  3. Unpause and trigger the `train_iris_model` DAG.
